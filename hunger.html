You should not watch and read the Hunger Games series. This cannot be expressed enough! The Hunger Games series revolves around a world full of divisions and hierarchy. Every year, 2 children from each of the 12 districts are picked at random to fight one another for the pleasure of the rich and the capital. They send the 24 children into an arena ,that is controlled by the capital, to kill one another using knives, bows and arrows, and anything that is resourceful in the land they are placed in. The capital forces the children to keep fighting one another until there is only one person left alive in the arena. This story is a view of what our modern-day can turn into. If our world gets too attached to this series, Earth can become a world full of wars and revolutions like the Hunger Games.
